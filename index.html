The Gun Control Act

The Gun control act of 1968 was put in place after the assassinations of President John Kennedy, Attorney General Robert Kennedy and Dr. Martin Luther King, Jr. The act banned mail order sales of rifles and shotguns and prohibiting most felons, drug users and people found mentally incompetent from buying guns
